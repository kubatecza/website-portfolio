who am I?

I am a Bachelor of Computer Science with specialization in computer systems and networks. I finished my studies in April of this year, I based my thesis on a web application designed by me for managing a car rental company. I spent the end of my studies writing in JavaScript, back then I decided that I would like my career to go in this direction. After graduation, I needed a job, so I started working as an IT Specialist near my town. I have had time to deal with work in my profession and in a typical office. After six months, I figured this was the right time to finally start doing what I wanted to do - become a developer.
I am strictly focused on learning and development. Currently working on my portfolio and looking for an internship or Junior Developer job to enter the world of developers.
